Long Term Care Pharmacy Services - Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our specialized pharmacy services for assisted living facilities

Service Overview

We provide comprehensive pharmacy services including prescription dispensing, medication packaging (blister packs and unit-dose), MAR preparation, medication therapy management, consultant pharmacist services, emergency medication kits, controlled substance management, on-site vaccinations, and staff training.

Yes, we serve assisted living facilities, memory care units, senior communities, nursing homes, and other long-term care providers throughout Maryland. Our services are customized to meet the specific needs and regulations for each facility type.

Absolutely. We maintain full HIPAA compliance and execute comprehensive Business Associate Agreements with all facilities. We also comply with Maryland privacy laws including the Confidentiality of Medical Records Act and upcoming Maryland Online Data Privacy Act.

Technology & Integration

Yes, we maintain secure electronic interfaces with most eMAR and EMR systems used by assisted living facilities. All electronic transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard encryption, and we maintain backup systems to ensure continuity of services.

We prepare and maintain comprehensive MARs that comply with Maryland COMAR regulations. Our electronic systems integrate with your facility's documentation, and we provide both electronic and paper backup records as needed.

Clinical Services

Our MTM services include comprehensive medication reviews, drug interaction screening, therapeutic optimization, clinical intervention documentation, and coordination with prescribers. We provide medication regimen reviews for new admissions within 72 hours and conduct regular reviews as agreed.

Yes, our licensed consultant pharmacists conduct regular facility visits, comprehensive medication reviews, participate in quality assurance programs as required by COMAR 10.34.36, and provide clinical recommendations to optimize resident care.

Emergency & Support Services

We provide 24/7/365 on-call pharmacist consultation, emergency medication delivery within 4 hours, emergency medication kit management, and immediate response to drug recalls. Our pharmacists are available around the clock for clinical consultations.

We maintain DEA-compliant procedures for controlled substance dispensing, storage, and documentation. We provide controlled substance destruction services with proper witness documentation and support facility diversion prevention programs. All controlled substances are reported to Maryland's PDMP within 24 hours.

We provide comprehensive staff training on medication administration, proper storage procedures, error prevention, regulatory compliance, and emergency protocols. When providing vaccination services, we ensure staff maintain required certifications per Maryland requirements.

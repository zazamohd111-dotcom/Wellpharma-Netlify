Seniors in Pikesville, Columbia, and throughout Baltimore County can now receive comprehensive medication management without leaving home.

WellPharma is proud to offer Pharmacy Care at Home services, providing specialized medication management and delivery to patients who are homebound or have difficulty managing complex medication regimens.

Patient Eligibility Criteria

Who Qualifies for Pharmacy Care at Home?

  • Homebound Medicare patients
  • Patients with multiple chronic diseases
  • Individuals taking 8+ medications
  • Those with difficulty managing medications independently
  • Patients at risk for hospital readmission
  • Individuals with complex medication regimens

Medicare Coverage

  • Certain Medicare insurance plans cover these CMS-approved services under Medicare Part D
  • Coverage varies by plan, and we'll verify your specific benefits to determine eligibility
  • Clinical pharmacy consultations covered
  • Medication therapy management included
  • Care coordination services provided

Benefits of Pharmacy Care at Home

Prevent Hospital Readmissions

Our clinical pharmacy services include medication adherence monitoring and care coordination to reduce complications that lead to hospital visits.

Improve Medication Adherence

Organized medication packaging and regular check-ins help ensure patients take medications correctly and on schedule.

Age in Place Safely

Comprehensive support allows seniors to remain in their homes while receiving professional medication management.

Cost-Effective Care

Preventing medication errors and hospital readmissions can result in significant healthcare cost savings.

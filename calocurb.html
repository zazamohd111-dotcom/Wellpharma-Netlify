The Science Behind Amarasate®

Breakthrough natural ingredient for appetite control

After studying over 1,000 plant compounds, researchers discovered that New Zealand bitter hops extract produced the strongest appetite-suppressing effect. This clinically studied ingredient, called Amarasate® (meaning "bitter satiation"), works by activating bitter taste receptors in your small intestine, naturally triggering powerful satiety hormones that reduce hunger, control cravings, and decrease caloric intake.

Natural Hormone Activation

Amarasate® activates three key satiety hormones simultaneously: GLP-1, CCK, and PYY — the same mechanisms targeted by prescription weight-loss medications, but through a natural, plant-based pathway.

How It Works

Natural appetite control through your body's own satiety system

The Mechanism

Delayed-release capsules reach your small intestine where they activate bitter taste receptors (TAS2R), triggering the release of GLP-1, CCK, and PYY. These hormones signal your brain that you're satisfied, helping you eat less without feeling deprived.

Effects are felt within 30 minutes to 2 hours of your first dose.

Unlike prescription medications that introduce synthetic peptides, Calocurb works through your intestinal bitter taste receptors to trigger your natural satiety response. Most people notice appetite reduction within 30 minutes to 2 hours of their first dose. By weeks 1-2, expect noticeable reductions in hunger and cravings. By weeks 3-4, you'll see measurable weight management progress as new eating patterns become established.

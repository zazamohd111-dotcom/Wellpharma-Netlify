Testing Services - Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our rapid testing services

General Testing Information

No appointment is necessary! We welcome walk-ins during our regular business hours. However, you can schedule an appointment online for faster service and guaranteed availability during busy times.

Rapid tests provide results in 15-30 minutes. Strep tests are ready in 10-15 minutes, flu tests in 15-20 minutes, and COVID rapid tests in 15-30 minutes. PCR COVID tests take 24-48 hours for results.

Rapid tests are highly accurate when performed correctly. Rapid flu tests are 50-70% sensitive and over 90% specific. Rapid strep tests are about 95% accurate. COVID rapid antigen tests are 85-95% accurate for symptomatic individuals. Our pharmacist is trained to ensure proper testing technique for the most reliable results.

Specific Tests

Rapid antigen tests provide results in 15-30 minutes and are best for detecting active infections when you have symptoms. PCR tests are more sensitive, can detect infection earlier, and are required for some travel or official documentation. PCR results take 24-48 hours but are considered the gold standard for COVID testing.

Get tested if you have flu symptoms (fever, body aches, cough, fatigue) and are at higher risk for complications, need to return to work or school quickly, or want antiviral treatment. Antivirals work best when started within 48 hours of symptom onset, so early testing is important.

Yes, we provide testing services for children ages 3 and up. Our pharmacist has experience working with pediatric patients to make the testing process as comfortable as possible. For children under 3, we recommend consulting with your pediatrician.

Cost & Insurance

Testing costs vary by test type and insurance coverage. Many insurance plans cover testing when medically necessary. Self-pay options are affordable and competitive with urgent care pricing. Call us for specific pricing information.

Yes! We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurance. We also accept HSA and FSA cards. Contact us with your insurance information and we'll verify your coverage before testing.

COVID testing coverage depends on your insurance plan and the reason for testing. Many plans still cover COVID testing when medically necessary or for diagnostic purposes. Contact us to check your specific coverage, or we can provide affordable self-pay options.

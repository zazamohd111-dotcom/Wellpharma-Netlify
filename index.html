Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pharmacy services in Randallstown, MD

Birth Control Services

Yes! Licensed pharmacists in Maryland can prescribe hormonal contraceptives after a consultation. No doctor referral needed. We offer same-day birth control prescriptions for $25.

Our pharmacist birth control consultation is only $25 - lower than competitors ($28-$39). Insurance may cover the consultation fee. The consultation includes personalized recommendations and same-day prescription.

We can prescribe all forms of hormonal contraceptives including birth control pills, patches, rings, and emergency contraception. Our pharmacist will recommend the best option based on your health history and preferences.

No insurance required! Our $25 consultation fee is affordable without insurance. We also accept most insurance plans that may cover both the consultation and prescription costs.

Compounding Services

A compounding pharmacy creates custom medications tailored to your specific needs. We can adjust dosages, remove allergens, change flavors for children, or create unique formulations not available commercially.

We compound a wide range of medications including pain management creams, pediatric formulations, veterinary medications, hormone replacement therapy, and allergen-free formulations. Each compound is made specifically for your prescription.

Yes, we specialize in custom hormone replacement therapy compounds. We can create personalized HRT formulations in various strengths and delivery methods based on your doctor's prescription and your individual needs.

Absolutely! We can compound medications without common allergens like gluten, lactose, dyes, or preservatives. This is especially helpful for patients with multiple allergies or sensitivities to commercial medication ingredients.

Immunizations & Vaccines

We offer flu shots, COVID-19 vaccines and boosters, pneumonia vaccines (Pneumovax and Prevnar), and shingles vaccines. All vaccines are administered by certified pharmacists.

Flu shot costs vary by insurance coverage. Most insurance plans cover flu shots at 100% with no copay. For uninsured patients, we offer competitive pricing. Call us for current pricing information.

Yes, we welcome walk-ins for most vaccines during business hours! While appointments are recommended to minimize wait times, we'll do our best to accommodate walk-in requests for flu shots and other routine vaccines.

Yes! We offer both pneumonia vaccines (Pneumovax23 and Prevnar) and shingles vaccine (Shingrix). These are especially important for adults over 50. We'll help determine which vaccines you need and when.

Prescription Services

You can refill prescriptions by calling (410) 521-WELL or visiting us in person. We're working on online refill capabilities. For now, phone refills are quick and easy - we can often have your medication ready within hours.

Yes! Prescription transfers are easy and free. Just bring your prescription bottles or call us with your current pharmacy information. We'll handle the transfer process and often have your medications ready the same day.

Yes! We offer free prescription delivery to Randallstown, Pikesville, Owings Mills, Reisterstown, Woodlawn, and Windsor Mill. Same-day delivery available for orders placed before 2:00 PM.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurance plans. We also accept HSA and FSA payments. Call us to verify your specific insurance coverage.

Pharmacy Care at Home Services

CMS-approved Medicare pharmacy services that help seniors age safely at home with comprehensive medication management and delivery. Our clinical pharmacists work with your healthcare team to optimize your medications.

Certain Medicare insurance plans cover these CMS-approved services under Medicare Part D. Coverage varies by plan, so we'll verify your specific benefits and help determine eligibility.

Medicare patients who are homebound, have multiple chronic diseases, take 8+ medications, or have difficulty managing their medications independently - and whose Medicare plans cover these services.

Medication therapy management, blister pack packaging, medication synchronization, clinical pharmacy consultations, and coordination with your healthcare team to prevent complications and hospital readmissions.

Our clinical pharmacy services include medication adherence monitoring, drug interaction screening, and care coordination to reduce complications that lead to hospital visits. We help seniors stay healthy at home.

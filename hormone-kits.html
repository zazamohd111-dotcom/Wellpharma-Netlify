Frequently Asked Questions

Which tier is right for me?

+

Hormone Baseline ($245) is perfect if you're just starting to understand your hormone health. Hormone Action Plan ($325) - our most popular option - is ideal if you want comprehensive testing with a personalized protocol and doctor coordination support. Complete Hormone Solution ($400) is best for those who want the most thorough testing available, including a free supplement, follow-up consultation, and priority support.

What's the difference between the test panels?

+

Our Hormone Baseline uses Profile II (E2, Pg, T, DS, Cx4) for core hormone assessment. Hormone Action Plan includes Profile III with additional estrogen metabolites (E1, E3) and ratio analysis for deeper insights. Complete Hormone Solution uses the LCM7 panel - our most comprehensive saliva test that analyzes all major hormones plus their metabolites for the complete picture.

How accurate are at-home hormone tests?

+

Our hormone test kits from ZRT Laboratory use CLIA-certified lab processing, providing the same accuracy as tests ordered by your doctor. The saliva collection method has been validated through extensive research to ensure reliable results.

Do I need a prescription for hormone testing?

+

No prescription is required. As a licensed pharmacy, we can provide these comprehensive hormone testing packages directly to you. Dr. Zahraa Babiker, PharmD will review your results with you and can provide a doctor coordination letter if you decide to pursue hormone replacement therapy.

How long does it take to get results?

+

Once ZRT Lab receives your sample, results are typically available within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a comprehensive report with your hormone levels and reference ranges. Dr. Babiker will then schedule your consultation to review the results and create your personalized plan.

Can I use my FSA/HSA to pay for testing?

+

Yes! All three tiers are FSA/HSA eligible. Simply use your FSA/HSA card at checkout. We provide detailed receipts that meet all requirements for reimbursement. The all-inclusive pricing means no surprise lab bills later.

Do you ship nationwide?

+

Yes! We ship hormone test kits nationwide. Your kit includes all collection materials, detailed instructions, and a prepaid return shipping label. Virtual consultations are available for all clients regardless of location, with in-person consultations available at our Randallstown, MD pharmacy.

What happens after I order?

+

After ordering, your test kit ships directly to you. Collect your sample at home following the simple instructions, then mail it back using the prepaid label. Once results are ready, Dr. Babiker will contact you to schedule your consultation. She'll review your results, answer questions, and provide personalized recommendations based on your tier.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are at-home hormone tests?

+

Our hormone test kits from ZRT Laboratory and Designs for Health use CLIA-certified lab processing, providing the same accuracy as tests ordered by your doctor. The saliva and blood spot collection methods have been validated through extensive research to ensure reliable results.

Do I need a prescription for hormone testing?

+

No prescription is required for our hormone test kits. As a licensed pharmacy, we can provide these tests directly to you. Our pharmacist is available for consultation to help you select the right test and understand your results.

How long does it take to get results?

+

Once the lab receives your sample, results are typically available within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a comprehensive report with your hormone levels, reference ranges, and interpretive information to help you understand your results.

Can I use my FSA/HSA to pay for testing?

+

Yes, hormone test kits are typically eligible for FSA/HSA reimbursement as they are considered medical diagnostic tests. We provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your FSA/HSA administrator for reimbursement.

What's included with my test kit purchase?

+

Each kit includes all collection materials, detailed instructions, prepaid return shipping label, lab processing, comprehensive results report, and a free consultation with our licensed pharmacist to discuss your results and answer any questions.
